rC3 - Loading Remote Chaos Experience

This year, CCC hosts the Remote Chaos Experience (rC3) instead of an on-site event in Leipzig.

Save the date: 27–30 December 2020

Updates

  • 2020-12-20: howto maschinenraum and howto play are online!
  • 2020-12-19: infodesk goes online!
  • 2020-12-15: rC3 is sold out.
  • 2020-12-08: There's a new ticket batch!
  • 2020-12-07: There's now an english translation of the maps howto!
  • 2020-12-03: rC3 is currently sold out. If there will be more tickets needs to be discussed in the next few days. Visit events.ccc.de on a regular basis to stay up to date!
  • 2020-12-02: There's now a place for shared tiles! See the howto or directly visit tiles.rc3.world.

Questions

Infodesk goes online!

During the warm up phase, you can already reach us at infodesk@rc3.world.
We will also aggregate your questions and publish them on the Infodesk subpage.

Additional communication channels will be available starting from day 1 (27 December), check back in a bit for details.
